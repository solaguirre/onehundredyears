Jardín de Sueños ~ Dream Garden

Please join use in celebrating Mission View's 100-year Anniversary of providing dreams and education to the children of South Tucson by including the name of soneone you wish to honor in a mural in our school garden "Jardín de Sueños".

Mitotera Gallery will be working with Mission View Students to create a painted mural that will include flowers, butterflies, and hummingbirds.

The name you wish to include will be painted into the mural to commemorate and remember someone who contributed to Mission View during the past 100 years. The unveiling ceremony will be on the evening of our 100-year celebration on Friday, March 10th, 2023. Please complete the form below if you wish to purchase a name to be painted on the Recuérdame Mission View Mural, deadline for submission is TBA.

You can add one persons name, Example: "Sandra Calkins" or you can add a family name, Example: "Familia Robles". Each name to be added to the mural will cost $100. If you wish to purchase, complete the form and send your payment via Venmo to @missionviewpta. If you wish to pay cash, please stop by the office for assitance.

Porfavor complete la solicitud para donar y agregar su nombre al Recuérdame Mural. Al completar la forma puede incluir el nombre de una persona individual, por ejemplo: "Sandra Calkins" o el nombre de familia, por ejemplo: "Familia Robles". El costo para agregar cada nombre es de $100.00. Si desea comprar un lugar en el mural para su nombre, complete la forma y mande su pago via Venmo a @missionviewpta. Si desea pagar en efectivo por favor visite la oficina durante las horas de escuela para recibir ayuda.
